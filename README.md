# Chado-Matcha-Journal-Application
An app where you can add entries of matcha places you like or dislike and give them ratings!

# Background
I really like matcha, so I decided to create an application that lets me add matcha places and rate them! Originally, I just wanted the app to just be for an individual to log their thoughts on a matcha place they tried out/go to and look back at, but I'm still deciding whether or not I want the app to let other users to see their ratings of the places they went to as well.

# How It Works
1. Once the app is launched, a user may either see an empty or filled dashboard that has matcha rating entries and may filter the results.
2. If a user wants to add an entry, they must create an account or log-in to their existing account.
3. Navigate to the "+ Add Entry" button on the top right corner.
4. Fill out the required spaces for your entry.
5. Once done, a user will be able to see their entry and can also delete it later on if they wish.
